# forage_Data-Labelling
Welcome to the Forage Data Labeling Simulation! A hands-on journey into the world of modern AI data operations. You'll also review pre-labeled items, make Keep/Fix/Flag calls with brief justifications, and propose targeted improvements to the guidelines, just like real teams do.

Intro & Scenario
Meet your team members and learn about your project goals.
Your Role
Entry-level Data Labeler on a help center AI project
Apply a small schema: Intent, Sentiment, and PII Flag
Use brief rationales for designated edge cases
Note ambiguities and propose one guideline clarification
Your Goal
Produce clear, consistent labels for a small batch of messages
Practice privacy-aware judgment by identifying and flagging PII
Build confidence with guidelines so similar items get similar labels
Prepare for basic reviewer decisions you’ll practice later in the simulation
